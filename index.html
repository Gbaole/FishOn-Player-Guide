How Do I Fish?

To begin, right-click to cast your rod into a body of water. After waiting for a short time, a fish will approach your bobber. When this occurs, the screen will display the text "Bite!" and you will hear a splashing sound. At this time, right-click once to hook the fish.

This will begin the fish-catching minigame. To complete the minigame and catch the fish, the player must prevent the right edge of the blue bar from hitting either the left or right side of the minigame popup, while keeping the right edge of the blue bar within the yellow rectangle for a short amount of time.

Above the blue bar, the green bar indicates how close the player is to catching the fish. The green bar increases rapidly when the right side edge of the blue bar is within the yellow rectangle. If the player manages to fully fill the green bar, they will catch the fish.

Basic Stuff

  • How to upgrade your rod: Every player has only one rod. You can upgrade the parts by completing locations or collections. Upgrade parts can also be purchased at Spawn from the Artisan (located on the left side).

    The Artisan will keep crafting parts while you're offline. Once completed, follow these steps to apply the parts:
    1. Hold your rod in your hand.
    2. Press Q to drop it (or right-click in your inventory).
    3. The Rod GUI will appear.
    4. Place the upgrade parts into the appropriate slots.
  • How to use bait/ lure: Open the rod GUI the same way you would to upgrade your rod. Place the bait or lure in the tackle box on the right. Read more about bait and lures on the official wiki.
  • How to get a pet: You can get a pet by fishing at every location. Pets have a chance to drop when you catch a fish. Each location has a different pet. Pets have a rarity value that progresses from common, rare, epic, legendary, and mythical. A pet's rarity can be raised by merging two level 100 pets with the same Pet Type and Rarity. The resulting merged pet will be 1 rarity level higher than the merged pets and their stats will be combined and averaged. Here is a Pet merge tool to calculate what you will get after you merge them. Read more about pets on the official wiki. Pet Merge Tool. The owner of the Pet Merge Tool is DannyPX / Matsu on Discord.
  • How to sell your fish: Every location has a fish merchant you can sell to and a location npc for you to tp to others locations
  • What is shard used for and How do you can craft armor: Players can fish up shards at all locations. Every location has a climate. Shard is determined by the climate of the location. You can craft armor with the shard you get like normal minecraft but no helmet. You can upgrade your armor up to tier 5. All armor can be upgraded at the Forger NPC (located near the Artisan NPC) with shards, which will upgrade its tier by 1, up to a maximum of 5. You will need to indentify it at Indentifier NPC (located near the Artisan NPC) to see the quality of the armor. And you can scrap it at the scrapper NPC (located behind Indentifier and forge NPC) to get some shards back. The color of the armor changes slightly with every tier. When you craft new armor it is tier 1. From then tier 2 will need 16 shards, tier 3 will need 48 shards, 96 shards for tier 4, and 256 shards for tier 5.
  • What is /quests: Quests require players to catch a certain amount of fish of one rarity or size. They may provide money, XP, location XP, pets, and shards when completed. The amount of fish needed per quest goes up with the player's level.

    Quests come in three difficulties which can be selected by the player: Easy, Medium, and Hard. Requirements, rewards, and abandon cost vary by difficulty. Easy quests are available at any level, Medium quests require level 20, and Hard quests require level 50.

    Read more about quests on the official wiki.
